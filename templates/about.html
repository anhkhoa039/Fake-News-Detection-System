What is FakeOff?

FakeOff is our initiative to combat misinformation by leveraging AI-driven analysis to evaluate the credibility of news content. This project provides real-time assessments, confidence scoring, sentiment insights, and more to help readers make informed decisions. The current application, "Fake News Detection", is powered by our FakeOff research and tooling.
